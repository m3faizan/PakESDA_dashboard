<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to create a Pakistan Intelligence Monitoring tool. The application is a dashboard built from scratch using a React, FastAPI, and MongoDB stack.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- A dashboard focused on Pakistan-related intelligence.
- **Live News Feed:** From multiple Pakistani sources, filterable by category, showing news from the last 48 hours.
- **News Ticker:** A fast-scrolling ticker at the top with news headlines.
- **Economic Panel:** Live data for various economic indicators. Most indicators are clickable to show a historical chart in a modal. This has been expanded to include:
    - PKR/USD, KSE-100, Gold Reserves, Forex Reserves (Total, SBP, Bank), Current Account Balance, Imports, Exports, Remittances, and Liquid Foreign Exchange Reserves.
- **Inflation Monitor Panel:** A dedicated panel for CPI (YoY and MoM) with a detailed historical chart showing base year changes.
- **Infrastructure Panels:** Separate panels for Air Traffic, Marine Traffic, and general infrastructure (Power Grid, Internet).
- **Governance Panel:** Shows statistics on projects completed by provincial chief ministers (currently static).
- **Road Advisory Panel:** Shows the latest travel advisories from the National Highways & Motorway Police (NHMP).
- **Data Persistence:** Historical data for airport and port traffic should be stored for future admin review.

**User's preferred language**: English
